## 2026-04-04 — Pre-Made Espresso Deep Research

**Completed foundational research on pre-made/batch-made espresso.**

Key findings written to: `memory/2026-04-04-premade-espresso-research.md`

**The Pivotal Discovery:**
Barista Hustle blind taste test revealed panelists **preferred pre-batched espresso in milk drinks** — smoother, less acrid crema taste.

**Key Players:**
- **Kirk Pearson** (Project Zero, Melbourne): 500+ coffees/day using pre-made espresso
- **Matt Perger** (Barista Hustle): Championed the method after blind testing
- **Jem Challender** (Barista Hustle): Contributed to tasting methodology

**Historical Context:**
- Moriondo's 1884 machine was a batch brewer (30 coffees at once)
- Original "espresso" (1869) meant strong coffee — not single-serve
- Single-serve is a 120-year marketing innovation, not a quality principle

**Real-World Proof:**
- Project Zero: Zero customer complaints about pre-made espresso
- Shelf life: Up to 96 hours refrigerated
- Method: Store in opaque sauce bottles, standard refrigeration

**The Controversy:**
Pre-made espresso breaks the "expres pour vous" (made especially for you) tradition, but blind tests prove it can be superior in milk drinks.

**Next Step:** Deploy daily monitoring system for ongoing intelligence gathering.
