## OPERATIONAL NOTES

### **Space Management (The Rotation Strategy)**
- **Daily Specials** rotate to keep variety without crowding the menu
- Only **7-8 items active per day** (3 bundles + 4 staples + 1-2 daily specials)
- Reduces decision paralysis, speeds up service

### **Prep Schedule**
| Day | Prep Focus | Transport |
|-----|-----------|-----------|
| Sunday | N/A | Full stock for Monday |
| Monday | Tuesday prep | Tuesday items |
| Tuesday | Wednesday prep | Wednesday items |
| Wednesday | Thursday prep | Thursday items |
| Thursday | Friday prep | Friday items |
| Friday | N/A | Weekend rest |

### **The "Free 100" Conversion Script**
**Morning Rush (7:45-10am):**
> Staff: "Free coffee! Which combo — Early Bird for 10, Big Breakfast for 12, or Power Lunch for 14?"

**Lunch Rush (12-2pm):**
> Staff: "Today's special is [Daily Item]. Coffee combo for 14, or just the food?"

### **Upsell Scripts**
- "Add a cookie for 6?" (impulse at register)
- "Soup and toastie combo for 14?" (increase basket size)
- "Make it a large coffee for 2 more?"

## MENU REFINEMENT RULES

**After Week 1:**
- If any daily special sells <15 units, replace it
- If any bundle sells <35 units, adjust price or offering

**After Month 1:**
- Identify the 2-3 best daily specials and consider making them permanent
- Cut the worst performing daily special entirely

**Seasonal Adjustments:**
- Monsoon season: More soup options
- Ramadan: Adjust hours, add iftar-friendly items
- Year-end: Holiday flavors (pumpkin spice, etc.)
