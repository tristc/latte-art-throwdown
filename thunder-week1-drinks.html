THUNDER CAFE — WEEK 1 SIGNATURE BEVERAGES

14 Cold Signature Mocktails & Coffee Drinks
Price Point: MYR 10 | Target COGS: <20% (under MYR 2)

COFFEE-BASED BEVERAGES (7 drinks)

1. PANDAN COLD BREW FIZZ

Description: Malaysian pandan meets sparkling cold brew — refreshing and aromatic

Ingredients: - 150ml cold brew concentrate (batch made) — MYR 0.40 - 20ml homemade pandan syrup — MYR 0.15 - 100ml soda water — MYR 0.20 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Pandan leaf garnish — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 0.85 (8.5%)

Steps: 1. Fill glass with ice 2. Add pandan syrup 3. Pour cold brew concentrate 4. Top with soda water 5. Garnish with fresh pandan leaf 6. Serve with metal straw

Batch Prep: Cold brew concentrate made weekly; pandan syrup made in bulk (simmer pandan leaves with gula melaka)

2. ESPRESSO TONIC MALAYSIA

Description: Classic espresso tonic with calamansi twist

Ingredients: - 2 shots espresso (pre-made batch) — MYR 0.50 - 150ml tonic water — MYR 0.60 - 10ml calamansi juice — MYR 0.10 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Calamansi slice garnish — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 1.30 (13%)

Steps: 1. Fill tall glass with ice 2. Squeeze calamansi juice 3. Pour tonic water 4. Slowly layer espresso over ice (creates cascade effect) 5. Garnish with calamansi wheel 6. Serve immediately

Batch Prep: Pre-pull espresso shots, store refrigerated up to 96 hours

3. GULA MELAKA COLD BREW LATTE

Description: Indonesian/Malaysian palm sugar with creamy cold brew

Ingredients: - 120ml cold brew concentrate — MYR 0.35 - 80ml fresh milk — MYR 0.35 - 25ml gula melaka syrup — MYR 0.25 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Gula melaka chunk garnish — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 1.05 (10.5%)

Steps: 1. Drizzle gula melaka syrup inside glass 2. Fill with ice 3. Pour cold brew concentrate 4. Float fresh milk on top (creates ombre effect) 5. Garnish with small gula melaka chunk 6. Stir before drinking

Batch Prep: Gula melaka syrup made in bulk (simmer palm sugar with water 1:1)

4. ROSE BANDUNG COLD BREW

Description: Malaysian bandung flavors meet coffee — floral and creamy

Ingredients: - 100ml cold brew concentrate — MYR 0.30 - 50ml rose syrup — MYR 0.20 - 80ml fresh milk — MYR 0.35 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Rose petals (dried) — MYR 0.10

Total COGS: MYR 1.00 (10%)

Steps: 1. Add rose syrup to glass 2. Fill with ice 3. Pour cold brew 4. Layer milk on top 5. Garnish with dried rose petals 6. Serve with straw

Batch Prep: Use commercial rose syrup (distributor pricing) or make in-house

5. YUZU ESPRESSO SPRITZ

Description: Japanese citrus meets Malaysian espresso — bright and zesty

Ingredients: - 2 shots espresso (pre-made) — MYR 0.50 - 30ml yuzu juice/puree — MYR 0.40 - 100ml sparkling water — MYR 0.20 - 10ml simple syrup — MYR 0.05 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Orange twist — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 1.25 (12.5%)

Steps: 1. Fill glass with ice 2. Add yuzu juice and simple syrup 3. Pour sparkling water 4. Layer espresso slowly over bar spoon 5. Garnish with orange twist 6. Serve with stirrer

Batch Prep: Pre-pull espresso shots; buy yuzu puree in bulk

6. COCONUT COLD BREW COLADA

Description: Tropical coconut cream with cold brew — dessert in a cup

Ingredients: - 120ml cold brew concentrate — MYR 0.35 - 60ml coconut cream — MYR 0.35 - 15ml simple syrup — MYR 0.05 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Toasted coconut flakes — MYR 0.10

Total COGS: MYR 0.90 (9%)

Steps: 1. Blend coconut cream with simple syrup 2. Fill glass with ice 3. Pour cold brew 4. Float coconut cream mixture on top 5. Garnish with toasted coconut flakes 6. Serve with wide straw

Batch Prep: Pre-mix coconut cream and sugar; cold brew concentrate weekly

7. MATCHA ESPRESSO FUSION (Dirty Matcha)

Description: Layered matcha and espresso — two caffeine sources, one drink

Ingredients: - 1 shot espresso (pre-made) — MYR 0.25 - 3g matcha powder — MYR 0.50 - 60ml water (for matcha) — MYR 0.02 - 60ml fresh milk — MYR 0.25 - 15ml vanilla syrup — MYR 0.10 - Ice — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 1.17 (11.7%)

Steps: 1. Whisk matcha with hot water until smooth 2. Add vanilla syrup to glass 3. Fill with ice 4. Pour matcha 5. Float milk layer 6. Slowly pour espresso on top (creates 3 layers) 7. Serve without stirring (customer stirs themselves)

Batch Prep: Pre-pull espresso; pre-sift matcha

NON-COFFEE MOCKTAILS (7 drinks)

8. CALAMANSI LYCHEE FIZZ

Description: Malaysian citrus meets floral lychee — refreshing and bright

Ingredients: - 40ml calamansi juice — MYR 0.25 - 40ml lychee syrup — MYR 0.30 - 1 lychee (canned) — MYR 0.15 - 100ml soda water — MYR 0.20 - 15ml simple syrup — MYR 0.05 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Mint sprig — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 1.05 (10.5%)

Steps: 1. Muddle lychee in glass 2. Add calamansi juice, lychee syrup, simple syrup 3. Fill with ice 4. Top with soda water 5. Stir gently 6. Garnish with lychee and mint

Batch Prep: Squeeze calamansi in bulk daily; use canned lychee (long shelf life)

9. PANDAN LEMONADE

Description: Malaysian pandan twist on classic lemonade

Ingredients: - 40ml fresh lemon juice — MYR 0.20 - 30ml pandan syrup — MYR 0.20 - 25ml simple syrup — MYR 0.10 - 150ml soda water — MYR 0.30 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Lemon wheel — MYR 0.05 - Pandan leaf — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 0.95 (9.5%)

Steps: 1. Add lemon juice, pandan syrup, simple syrup to glass 2. Fill with ice 3. Top with soda water 4. Stir gently 5. Garnish with lemon wheel and pandan leaf

Batch Prep: Pandan syrup made in bulk; pre-squeeze lemons

10. PASSION FRUIT MINT COOLER

Description: Tropical passion fruit with fresh mint — thirst-quenching

Ingredients: - 50ml passion fruit puree — MYR 0.40 - 8-10 mint leaves — MYR 0.10 - 20ml simple syrup — MYR 0.10 - 150ml soda water — MYR 0.30 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Passion fruit seeds — MYR 0.10

Total COGS: MYR 1.05 (10.5%)

Steps: 1. Muddle mint with simple syrup in glass 2. Add passion fruit puree 3. Fill with ice 4. Top with soda water 5. Stir gently 6. Garnish with fresh passion fruit seeds and mint sprig

Batch Prep: Use frozen passion fruit puree (bulk pricing)

11. ROSE LYCHEE BANDUNG

Description: Classic Malaysian pink drink — creamy and floral

Ingredients: - 50ml rose syrup — MYR 0.20 - 40ml lychee syrup — MYR 0.30 - 150ml fresh milk — MYR 0.65 - 20ml condensed milk — MYR 0.10 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Rose petals — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 1.35 (13.5%)

Steps: 1. Add rose syrup and lychee syrup to glass 2. Add condensed milk 3. Fill with ice 4. Pour fresh milk 5. Stir well 6. Garnish with dried rose petals

Batch Prep: Pre-mix syrups for faster service

12. MANGO STICKY RICE MILK

Description: Dessert-inspired drink — mango and coconut cream

Ingredients: - 80ml mango puree — MYR 0.45 - 50ml coconut cream — MYR 0.30 - 30ml fresh milk — MYR 0.15 - 15ml sugar syrup — MYR 0.05 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Toasted rice flakes — MYR 0.10

Total COGS: MYR 1.10 (11%)

Steps: 1. Blend mango puree with coconut cream and sugar 2. Fill glass with ice 3. Pour mango mixture 4. Float fresh milk on top 5. Garnish with toasted rice flakes 6. Serve with spoon and straw

Batch Prep: Pre-blend mango base; toast rice flakes in advance

13. HIBISCUS LEMONADE (Roselle)

Description: Local roselle flower tea meets lemonade — tart and refreshing

Ingredients: - 150ml hibiscus tea (chilled) — MYR 0.20 - 30ml lemon juice — MYR 0.15 - 25ml simple syrup — MYR 0.10 - 100ml soda water — MYR 0.20 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Hibiscus flower — MYR 0.10

Total COGS: MYR 0.80 (8%)

Steps: 1. Brew hibiscus tea concentrate, chill 2. Add lemon juice and simple syrup to glass 3. Fill with ice 4. Pour hibiscus tea 5. Top with soda water 6. Garnish with dried hibiscus flower

Batch Prep: Brew hibiscus concentrate in bulk; keeps 3 days

14. BUTTERFLY PEA LIMEADE

Description: Color-changing blue tea with lime — Instagram star

Ingredients: - 200ml butterfly pea tea (chilled) — MYR 0.25 - 30ml lime juice — MYR 0.15 - 25ml simple syrup — MYR 0.10 - 50ml soda water — MYR 0.10 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Lime wheel — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 0.70 (7%)

Steps: 1. Brew butterfly pea tea, chill (naturally blue) 2. Fill glass with ice 3. Pour butterfly pea tea 4. Add simple syrup 5. Top with soda water 6. Serve with lime wedge on side (customer squeezes lime — drink changes from blue to purple!)

Batch Prep: Brew butterfly pea concentrate; dramatic color change creates shareable moment
