### 2. ESPRESSO TONIC MALAYSIA

**[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER - PHOTO 2]**
*Insert photo: Tall glass, dark espresso layer on top of clear tonic, ice cubes visible, calamansi wheel on rim, cascade effect visible*

**Visual Description:**
- **Color:** Dark espresso brown floating on crystal-clear tonic, gradient layers
- **Glass:** Tall Collins glass (350ml), full height
- **Garnish:** Calamansi wheel on rim (cut 3/4 through to sit on glass edge)
- **Ice:** Large clear ice cubes, single column
- **Effect:** Espresso slowly sinking through tonic (capture within 30 seconds of pour)
- **Background:** Dark wood or black slate for contrast
- **Props:** None (let the layers be the focus)
- **Lighting:** Backlighting to show effervescence and layers

**Description:** Classic espresso tonic with calamansi twist

**Ingredients:**
- 2 shots espresso (pre-made batch) — MYR 0.50
- 150ml tonic water — MYR 0.60
- 10ml calamansi juice — MYR 0.10
- Ice — MYR 0.05
- Calamansi slice garnish — MYR 0.05

**Total COGS:** MYR 1.30 (13%)

**Steps:**
1. Fill tall glass with ice
2. Squeeze calamansi juice
3. Pour tonic water
4. Slowly layer espresso over ice (creates cascade effect)
5. Garnish with calamansi wheel
6. Serve immediately

**Batch Prep:** Pre-pull espresso shots, store refrigerated up to 96 hours

### 7. MATCHA ESPRESSO FUSION (Dirty Matcha)

**[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER - PHOTO 7]**
*Insert photo: Mason jar, three distinct layers (green matcha bottom, white milk middle, brown espresso top), clean lines between layers*

**Visual Description:**
- **Color:** Three layers — vibrant green (bottom), white (middle), dark brown (top)
- **Glass:** Mason jar with handle (transparent to show layers)
- **Garnish:** None (layers are the visual focus)
- **Ice:** Large ice cubes holding layers apart
- **Layers:** Sharp, clean lines between each layer
- **Background:** Minimal white or concrete
- **Props:** None (minimalist)
- **Lighting:** Side lighting to highlight layer separation
- **Angle:** Straight-on at eye level

**Description:** Layered matcha and espresso — two caffeine sources, one drink

**Ingredients:**
- 1 shot espresso (pre-made) — MYR 0.25
- 3g matcha powder — MYR 0.50
- 60ml water (for matcha) — MYR 0.02
- 60ml fresh milk — MYR 0.25
- 15ml vanilla syrup — MYR 0.10
- Ice — MYR 0.05

**Total COGS:** MYR 1.17 (11.7%)

**Steps:**
1. Whisk matcha with hot water until smooth
2. Add vanilla syrup to glass
3. Fill with ice
4. Pour matcha
5. Float milk layer
6. Slowly pour espresso on top (creates 3 layers)
7. Serve without stirring (customer stirs themselves)

**Batch Prep:** Pre-pull espresso; pre-sift matcha
