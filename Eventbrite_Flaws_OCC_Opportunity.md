## The Flaws: Deep Dive

### 1. EXTORTIONATE FEE STRUCTURE (The Revenue Killer)

#### The Problem

Eventbrite's fees are devastating for small coffee events:

**2025 Eventbrite Fee Structure (US):**
- **Service fee:** 3.7% + $1.79 per paid ticket
- **Payment processing:** 2.9% of total order
- **Effective total:** ~6.6% of ticket price PLUS fixed fee

**Real-World Examples:**

| Ticket Price | Eventbrite Fees | You Receive | Fee % |
|--------------|-----------------|-------------|-------|
| $15 workshop | $1.34 + $0.44 = $1.78 | $13.22 | **11.9%** |
| $25 cupping | $1.72 + $0.73 = $2.45 | $22.55 | **9.8%** |
| $50 latte art class | $3.64 + $1.45 = $5.09 | $44.91 | **10.2%** |
| $100 training | $5.49 + $2.90 = $8.39 | $91.61 | **8.4%** |

**For a small coffee shop running a $25 latte art class for 20 people:**
- Gross revenue: $500
- Eventbrite fees: $49
- **You lose nearly 10% to Eventbrite**

**Hidden Fees Organizers Discover Too Late:**
- QR code scanning: $0.25 per scan (500 attendees = $125)
- "Premium" support: $199/month for phone support
- Reserved seating: Additional fees
- Marketing tools: Upsells at every turn

#### Coffee Industry Impact

Small coffee businesses operate on thin margins:
- Independent cafés: 2–5% net profit margins
- Small roasters: 5–10% margins
- Losing 10% to Eventbrite is devastating

**Real organizer quote:** *"Five extra bucks on ticket sales can add up for a small organized event. It does not sound like a lot, but remember: it is an independent event organizer. Those fees add pain."*

**OCC Opportunity:**
- Flat fee pricing (e.g., $0.50–$1 per ticket, regardless of price)
- Or percentage capped at 3% max
- Free for community events under 50 people
- Coffee industry-focused; we understand thin margins

### 2. DELAYED PAYOUTS (The Cash Flow Crisis)

#### The Problem

Eventbrite holds your money hostage:

**Eventbrite Payout Policy:**
- Standard: **5 business days AFTER event concludes**
- New accounts: Additional holds possible
- Large events: Additional risk-based delays
- Bank transfer: Additional 3–5 business days

**Total wait: 1–2 weeks to access your own money**

**Coffee Industry Pain:**
- Cupping event Saturday → Get paid the following Friday (if lucky)
- Need to pay coffee supplier Monday? Too bad.
- Venue deposit due Tuesday? Bridge loan time.

**Real organizer quote:** *"If you are a lone creator, space-vibing influencer, or single-proprietorship small business, then you want to ensure the funds are in your account. That delay of a few days is important."*

**OCC Opportunity:**
- Daily payouts
- Or instant via Stripe Connect
- Pre-event payouts for established organizers
- Cash flow that actually works for coffee businesses

**OCC Opportunity:**

### 5. DATA OWNERSHIP ISSUES (Eventbrite Owns Your Audience)

#### The Problem

When you use Eventbrite, you're building Eventbrite's database, not yours:

**Eventbrite's Data Practices:**
- Eventbrite retains all attendee data
- Uses data for advertising and recommendations
- Attendees marketed to competing events
- Export limitations
- No ownership of your own customer relationships

**Coffee Industry Impact:**
- Can't build direct relationships with your customers
- Can't market your next event without Eventbrite
- No customer insights or analytics ownership
- Competing roasters/cafés can advertise to YOUR attendees

**Real organizer quote:** *"Ticketbud gave me full ownership of attendee data. Ensuring my attendee data is not resold or used for ads and I remain in full control."*

**OCC Opportunity:**
- Organizer owns all attendee data
- Direct communication with attendees
- No competing advertising
- GDPR-compliant data export
- Build your own coffee community
