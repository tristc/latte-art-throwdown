## 1. Executive Summary

Espresso King is a **zero-capital-expenditure** food & beverage kiosk operating within a private office building. The unique business model eliminates traditional F&B costs (rent, electricity, setup) while capturing a **captive audience of 800+ daily office workers** with no internal competition.

| Metric | Value |

| **Location** | Menara UOA South Bangsar, KL |

| **Target Daily Transactions** | 140 orders/day |

## 5. Customer Segmentation Strategy

### Profile A — Budget (Target: 100 orders/day)
- **Price:** RM 4-10 per item
- **Target:** RM 10-15 combo spend
- **Products:** Coffee + pastry, sandwiches, simple meals
- **Strategy:** Volume play, subsidized pricing, grab-and-go

### Profile B — Mid (Target: 30 orders/day)
- **Price:** RM 10-25 per item
- **Target:** RM 20-25 combo spend
- **Products:** Premium sandwiches, salads, specialty drinks
- **Strategy:** Quality upgrades, meal deals

### Profile C — Premium (Target: 10 orders/day)
- **Price:** RM 25+ per item
- **Products:** Gourmet items, specialty coffee, premium combos
- **Strategy:** Margin maximization, exclusive offerings

## 6. Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competition
- **None inside the building** — monopoly position

### Indirect Competition (Outside)
- Nearest coffee: 10-minute walk to lobby exit
- External cafes: Compete for lunch crowd only
- Delivery apps: Time delay (30-45 min), delivery fees

### Why We Win
1. **Convenience:** 30 seconds vs 10 minutes
2. **Price:** Subsidized post-free-coffee pricing
3. **Time:** Golden hour capture (staff arriving 7:45-10am)
4. **Captive:** No need to leave the building
