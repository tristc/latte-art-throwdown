### Key Differentiators Established

**The Judging System (REVOLUTIONARY):**
- Comparison-based (LEFT/RIGHT) - never subjective scoring
- **Binary preference only** — no scoresheets, no deliberation possible
- Point allocation:
  - Latte Art: 3 points
  - Taste Balance: 1 point
  - Tactile: 1 point
  - Flavour: 1 point
  - Overall Preference: 5 points
  - Total: 11 points per judge — all binary choices

**The Consumer Sensory Protocol:**
- **One Objective Factor:** Smoothness (texture)
- **Four Subjective Factors:** Sweetness, Acidity, Flavor, Overall Preference

**The WEC Message:**
"There are no wrong answers. Your taste is valid. If you like it, it's good coffee. But, can the next coffee be better?"
