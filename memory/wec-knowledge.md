# World Espresso Championship (WEC) - Knowledge Base

## Overview
The World Espresso Championship (WEC) is a premier high-stakes competition that brings together the world's top coffee professionals to compete for the crown. Founded in 2022.

## Key Differentiators from WBC

### WEC Philosophy
- **Standardized Equipment**: All competitors use the SAME coffee, espresso machine, grinder, and water
- **Barista as Variable**: The barista is the ONLY changing variable - emphasizing extraction technique and repeatability
- **Elite Entry**: Only current national champions (Barista & Brewer) are eligible to participate
- **Accessible Recipes**: Winner's complete recipe is made public so anyone can replicate it at home/cafe

### WBC Philosophy (for contrast)
- **Bring Your Own**: Competitors choose their own coffee, often exotic/expensive beans (Geisha, etc.)
- **Signature Drinks**: Elaborate creative beverages are a major component
- **15-Minute Performance**: 4 espressos + 4 milk drinks + 4 signature drinks
- **Open to National Champions**: Qualifying path through national competitions

## Competition Format
- **Preliminary Rounds**: Earlier in day (competitors + supporting team only)
- **Public Finals**: Evening event with spectators
- **Top 8**: Battle in finals witnessed by public

The winning recipe is published with full details:
- Water Filtration
- Grinder
- Espresso Machine
- Barista Tools
- Green Coffee Beans
- Roast Profile
- Espresso Recipe
- Milk
- etc.

Goal: Anyone can make the championship coffee at home or in their cafe.
