# World Barista Championship (WBC) & SCA - Reference

## World Barista Championship (WBC)

### Overview
- Part of the **World Coffee Championships (WCC)** portfolio
- Run by **Specialty Coffee Association (SCA)**
- Premier competitive coffee event globally
- 25 editions completed as of 2025

### History
- **2000**: First WBC in Monte Carlo, Monaco - ~17 competitors, 500 attendees
- **2001**: Miami, USA - SCAE + SCAA collaboration began
- **2003**: Boston, USA - First broadcast on national TV (20+ million viewers)
- **2007**: First event outside US/Europe (Tokyo, Japan)
- Growth: From 17 competitors to 50+ Competition Bodies

### Competition Format
- **Duration**: 15-minute performance
- **Components**:
  - 4 espressos
  - 4 milk-based drinks
  - 4 signature beverages (creative/original)
- **Judging Criteria**:
  - Taste (flavor, aftertaste)
  - Cleanliness
  - Creativity
  - Technical proficiency
  - Overall presentation
- **Scoresheets**: Technical scores + 4 sensory scoresheets

- **2027**: Tokyo, Japan (20 years since last hosted there)

## Specialty Coffee Association (SCA)

### Overview
- **Full Name**: Specialty Coffee Association
- **Status**: Largest global coffee trade association
- **Mission**: Making coffee better by fostering a global coffee community
- **CEO**: Yannis Apostolopoulos

### Formation
- **2017**: Merger of SCAE (Specialty Coffee Association of Europe, est. 1998) + SCAA (Specialty Coffee Association of America)

### World Coffee Championships Portfolio (7 competitions)
1. World Barista Championship (WBC)
2. World Latte Art Championship
3. World Brewers Cup
4. World Coffee in Good Spirits Championship
5. World Cup Tasters Championship
6. World Coffee Roasting Championship
7. Cezve/Ibrik Championship

Plus: WCC All-Stars program, Espresso Bar, Brew Bar features

### Key Activities
- Research
- Standards development
- Education
- Events
- Community building
- Supporting "activities to make specialty coffee a thriving, equitable, and sustainable endeavor for the entire value chain"

## WEC vs WBC: Key Differences

| Aspect | WEC | WBC |
|--------|-----|-----|
| **Equipment** | Standardized (same for all) | Competitor's choice |
| **Coffee** | Same coffee for all competitors | Competitor chooses (often expensive/special) |
| **Format** | Head-to-head, extraction-focused | 15-min performance with signature drinks |
| **Entry** | National champions only | National champions through Competition Bodies |
| **Focus** | Technique, repeatability | Creativity, presentation, showmanship |
| **Winner's Recipe** | Published for public replication | Not typically published |
| **Organizer** | Independent (Dalla Corte partnership) | SCA (World Coffee Championships) |
| **Relation** | Complementary format | Established industry standard |
