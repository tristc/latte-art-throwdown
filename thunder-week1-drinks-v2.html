COFFEE-BASED BEVERAGES (7 drinks)

1. PANDAN COLD BREW FIZZ

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER - PHOTO 1] Insert photo: Tall highball glass, light amber liquid with bubbles, fresh pandan leaf garnish, metal straw, ice visible

Visual Description: - Color: Light golden-amber with effervescent bubbles rising - Glass: Tall highball (300ml), condensation on exterior - Garnish: Single fresh pandan leaf (15cm) inserted at 45-degree angle - Ice: Clear ice cubes, stacked to top - Background: Light wood or white marble - Props: Metal straw (gold or silver), minimal styling - Lighting: Bright, natural daylight from side

Description: Malaysian pandan meets sparkling cold brew — refreshing and aromatic

Ingredients: - 150ml cold brew concentrate (batch made) — MYR 0.40 - 20ml homemade pandan syrup — MYR 0.15 - 100ml soda water — MYR 0.20 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Pandan leaf garnish — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 0.85 (8.5%)

Steps: 1. Fill glass with ice 2. Add pandan syrup 3. Pour cold brew concentrate 4. Top with soda water 5. Garnish with fresh pandan leaf 6. Serve with metal straw

Batch Prep: Cold brew concentrate made weekly; pandan syrup made in bulk (simmer pandan leaves with gula melaka)

2. ESPRESSO TONIC MALAYSIA

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER - PHOTO 2] Insert photo: Tall glass, dark espresso layer on top of clear tonic, ice cubes visible, calamansi wheel on rim, cascade effect visible

Visual Description: - Color: Dark espresso brown floating on crystal-clear tonic, gradient layers - Glass: Tall Collins glass (350ml), full height - Garnish: Calamansi wheel on rim (cut 3/4 through to sit on glass edge) - Ice: Large clear ice cubes, single column - Effect: Espresso slowly sinking through tonic (capture within 30 seconds of pour) - Background: Dark wood or black slate for contrast - Props: None (let the layers be the focus) - Lighting: Backlighting to show effervescence and layers

Description: Classic espresso tonic with calamansi twist

Ingredients: - 2 shots espresso (pre-made batch) — MYR 0.50 - 150ml tonic water — MYR 0.60 - 10ml calamansi juice — MYR 0.10 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Calamansi slice garnish — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 1.30 (13%)

Steps: 1. Fill tall glass with ice 2. Squeeze calamansi juice 3. Pour tonic water 4. Slowly layer espresso over ice (creates cascade effect) 5. Garnish with calamansi wheel 6. Serve immediately

Batch Prep: Pre-pull espresso shots, store refrigerated up to 96 hours

3. GULA MELAKA COLD BREW LATTE

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER - PHOTO 3] Insert photo: Rocks glass, three-layer ombre effect (dark coffee bottom, caramel middle, white milk top), gula melaka chunk on top

Visual Description: - Color: Three distinct layers — dark brown (bottom), caramel amber (middle), white (top) - Glass: Short rocks glass (250ml), wide mouth to show layers - Garnish: Small cube of gula melaka on surface or rim - Ice: Clear ice cubes, filling 2/3 of glass - Layers: Visible ombre effect, not yet stirred - Background: Warm wood tones or beige marble - Props: Long spoon for stirring placed beside glass - Lighting: Soft, warm lighting to highlight caramel tones

Description: Indonesian/Malaysian palm sugar with creamy cold brew

Ingredients: - 120ml cold brew concentrate — MYR 0.35 - 80ml fresh milk — MYR 0.35 - 25ml gula melaka syrup — MYR 0.25 - Ice — MYR 0.05 - Gula melaka chunk garnish — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 1.05 (10.5%)

Steps: 1. Drizzle gula melaka syrup inside glass 2. Fill with ice 3. Pour cold brew concentrate 4. Float fresh milk on top (creates ombre effect) 5. Garnish with small gula melaka chunk 6. Stir before drinking

Batch Prep: Gula melaka syrup made in bulk (simmer palm sugar with water 1:1)

7. MATCHA ESPRESSO FUSION (Dirty Matcha)

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER - PHOTO 7] Insert photo: Mason jar, three distinct layers (green matcha bottom, white milk middle, brown espresso top), clean lines between layers

Visual Description: - Color: Three layers — vibrant green (bottom), white (middle), dark brown (top) - Glass: Mason jar with handle (transparent to show layers) - Garnish: None (layers are the visual focus) - Ice: Large ice cubes holding layers apart - Layers: Sharp, clean lines between each layer - Background: Minimal white or concrete - Props: None (minimalist) - Lighting: Side lighting to highlight layer separation - Angle: Straight-on at eye level

Description: Layered matcha and espresso — two caffeine sources, one drink

Ingredients: - 1 shot espresso (pre-made) — MYR 0.25 - 3g matcha powder — MYR 0.50 - 60ml water (for matcha) — MYR 0.02 - 60ml fresh milk — MYR 0.25 - 15ml vanilla syrup — MYR 0.10 - Ice — MYR 0.05

Total COGS: MYR 1.17 (11.7%)

Steps: 1. Whisk matcha with hot water until smooth 2. Add vanilla syrup to glass 3. Fill with ice 4. Pour matcha 5. Float milk layer 6. Slowly pour espresso on top (creates 3 layers) 7. Serve without stirring (customer stirs themselves)

Batch Prep: Pre-pull espresso; pre-sift matcha
