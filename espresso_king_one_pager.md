# Espresso King — Executive Summary
## Investor Meeting One-Pager

---

### What It Is
A **zero-capital-expenditure** coffee & food kiosk inside Menara UOA South Bangsar, KL. Client pays all setup costs; we operate with a captive audience of **800 office staff daily**.

---

### Why This Wins

✅ **No rent** — Client pays everything  
✅ **No setup cost** — Equipment provided  
✅ **No competition** — Monopoly inside the building  
✅ **Captive market** — 800 staff, 10-min walk to nearest exit  
✅ **Subsidized traffic** — 100 free coffees daily = guaranteed footfall
